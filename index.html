Welcome to Personal Trainer. I aim to provide a training plan personal to you so you can develop and reach your personal goals no matter your age or training experience.


Beginner

  • One on one training

  • Personal Trainer devices a beginners plan and schedule

  • 10 initial 1 hour sessions

  • At clients home or public park

  • Further sessions to be arranged/move to Intermediate level

Intermediate

  • One on one training

  • Training Plan agreed by both parties to meet specific training goals

  • 10 initial 1 hour 30 minute sessions

  • At clients home, public park or at gym depending on the needs

  • Further sessions to be arranged/move to Advanced level

Advanced

  • One on one training

  • Training Plan devised based on an upcoming event/goal

  • Sessions and timings to be agreed to achieve goal

  • At clients home, public park or at gym depending on the needs

  • Evaluation and future guidance

Group

  • Group training sessions up to maximum of 10 people

  • Suitable for an upcoming event such as a marathon or group challenge

  • Sessions and timings to be agreed to achieve goal

  • At public park or suitable public space depending on the needs

  • Evaluation and future guidance
